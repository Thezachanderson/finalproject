Enemy 1: Eye:
    Uses lasers, or ranged direct attack on player, with damage dealt (along with range?) scaling with difficulty
Enemy 2: Cloaked:
    Uses limited area of effect attack on player, with damage dealt scaling with difficulty
Enemy 3: Rodney the rat:
    Uses melee attack on player, with damage dealt scaling with difficulty

All enemies spawn at potential predetermined spots in rooms. They also will have an in room determined travel route
that will change to follow player when player is detected by that Enemy.
